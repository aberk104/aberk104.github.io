Monthly COVID-19 Cases Per 100,000 People by US State

This map shows the number of new COVID-19 cases each month for each state in the United States from January 2020 through July 2021. In order to compare across states with different populations, the number of monthly cases is normalized to a case rate per 100K people. The play, next, and previous buttons will animate the chart, showing how the US hot spots have changed each month over the last 1.5 years. The legend will change each month showing the highest and lowest case rates for that month. The legend title indicates the month being displayed on the chart.
